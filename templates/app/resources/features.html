Tubble Features

Tubble makes it fun and easy to create a digital bucket list. Tubble consists of destinations, each destination contains information such as city, country, type of adventure, activities and more. Below are some of the exciting features Tubble has to offer!

Create

Keep track of where you want to go and where you have been.

Details

Store relevant information such as flight time, activities, and notes.

Update

Simply update your destinations wherever you are.

Visited

Save past destinations by changing the status of a destination to Visited.

List

Destinations are organized by places you want to go and places you have visited.

Tubble It

Not sure where to go next? Tubble gives you suggestions based on your current destinations.

Search

Quickly retrieve destinations you have created.

Delete

Remove destinations if they become undesirable.
